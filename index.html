こんにちは！

Testing the documentation of the journey to go and be abroad as a CUNY Hunter Exchange student at Meiji Gakuin Univserity

Before you read

This resouce exists primarily as a means to better your understanding of the study abroad experience for the CUNY Hunter/Meiji Gakuin student exchange program. From the beginning processes to actually going abroad, I will document what I feel is necessary for you to understand what this program is like. However, I do not represent either CUNY Hunter or Meiji Gakuin in any way, shape, or form. I intend to offer my experiences on things such as my personal application process, preparing for actually going abroad, and being abroad. However, if you have questions pertaining to your personal application/financial aid, is it a conversation to be had with your advisors and NOT me.
